About

Our collections are exhaustively crafted and effortlessly worn,
tailored precisely for an era of comfortable indifference.
When you buy from GUILTY, you are not purchasing
a clean conscience, you are securing your piece of the machine.Wear it. Obsess over it. Own the guilt.

Every drop is an unspoken agreement.
We manufacture scarcity, and you validate it with a click.
We all know how the system works, yet we collectively
choose to look away.GUILTY doesn't offer a distraction from consumer culture;
it offers a premium space to finally own it.

GUILTY exists because you do.We operate on a simple truth: we all want more, and we want it now.
We no longer pretend to care about sustainability.
We design for the modern appetite, the insatiable desire
to possess beautiful things, regardless of the cost.

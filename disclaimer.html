GUILTY is a conceptual student project
created at Bezalel Academy of Arts and Design,
dissecting systemic patterns in the global
fashion industry. Statements on this site are generalized for a
design narrative, not verified data or research.
No specific brand, company, or factory is named
or implicated; the critique is systemic. This is not a commercial brand.
Nothing is for sale; all PRODUCTS DISPLAYED
are simulated for academic purposes only
and no real purchases can be made. The models featured are not connected to the
project's concept, critique, or content in any way.
